Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the format played?

    The format is handrum cricket tournament.

  • How do I ragistor?

    Each player has to register separately by clicking on the rego link provided on the competition page and brochure. The link is: https://playreg.cricket.com.au/pages/reg/welcome.aspx?redirect=1&type=8&id=37413&entityid=2880

  • How many games will each team get?

    We will endeavor to give at least 3 to 4 matches to each team plus the final

  • How many players in each team?

    9 to 11 players in each team. 9 fielders maximum at one time on the ground. 10 wickets mark close of an innings.

  • What if I don't have a team?

    Contact us with your details and we will endeavor to find you a team.

  • What times to game start & finish

    Game Start : 8:00 AM
    Game Finish : 5:00 PM

  • What equipments is provided?

    Teams are provided with one ball each match, score book and 2 set of stumps along with boundary markers that will be on the ground at all times. You need to have your own cricket kit.

  • What is ragistration fee?

    Fee Rs 350 per team.

  • What if I don't have enough players for a full team?

    If you do not have enough players for a full team, contact us with your current situation and numbers and we will merge teams in a similar situation as yours.
